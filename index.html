Drone Detection refers to the use of computer vision and AI techniques to automatically identify and locate drones in images or video feeds. This project uses AI and NLP to analyze real-time news and predict its impact on stock market prices. It combines news data with historical stock information to train a machine learning model for accurate forecasting. The system is automated with Apache Airflow and deployed via Gradio, enabling timely, scalable, and interactive insights for investors.
